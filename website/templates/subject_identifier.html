Subject Identifier

This is a tool which identifies which mathematical subjects a given article is. Fill in the title and/or abstract fields below to match a paper of interest, then after submission, you will get a list of recommended subjects that paper belongs to. The insert sample buttons will fill the given field with those of my PhD thesis. (Note that it can take a up to a minute to start up the recommender service if it hasn't been used recently.)


Thank you to arXiv for use of its open access interoperability.
